With an eye on present maintenance conditions of Shajiao C Power Plant " s induced fans ,  a way of planning an overall scheme of condition based maintenance is being proposed. The optimal maintenance strategy of induced fans is obtained by evaluating their conditions and assessing the risks concerned. Practical application of such a scheme to the induced fans of Shajiao C Power Plant " s 660MW generating set shows it to be fitting and facilitating maintenance. It moreover shows that the model " s reasoning is reliable ,  execution effects satisfactory ;  herewith offering for power plants a way of optimizing maintenance. Figs 2 ,  table 1 and refs 4.
